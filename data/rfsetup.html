How It Works

1. Learn RF Code: Click "Start Learning" and press your RF remote button.

2. Home Assistant Entity: An "RF Toggle" entity will appear alongside your relay switches.

3. Automatic Trigger: When the learned RF signal is received, the toggle turns ON for 2 seconds, then automatically turns OFF.

4. Use in Automations: Create Home Assistant automations based on this toggle state.

Technical Details

Receiver: SYN480R 433MHz

Pin: GPIO 15

Toggle Duration: 2 seconds

Storage: Code saved to NVS (persists across reboots)
